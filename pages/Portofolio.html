Our Work and Achievements

At Afya Mama, we are committed to making a difference in maternal and child healthcare. Here are some of our recent projects, partnerships, and initiatives aimed at improving health outcomes for mothers and children.

Maternal Health Outreach Program

Improving maternal health through education and outreach programs in remote areas, providing care to thousands of mothers.

Partnership with World Health Organization

Collaborating with WHO to bring essential resources and vaccines to underserved communities across Kenya.

Nutrition for Mothers Program

Helping mothers access nutritional guidance and resources, ensuring they receive the support needed for healthy pregnancies.

Child Immunization Drive

Partnered with local health providers to immunize over 5,000 children against preventable diseases.

Community Health Worker Training

Equipping community health workers with skills and knowledge to better serve mothers and children in their communities.

Support for Rural Health Clinics

Providing supplies and assistance to rural clinics, ensuring they are well-equipped to meet the needs of mothers and children.
